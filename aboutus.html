ABOUT US

RentEasy is a service of furniture renting for everyone. The aim of this service is to allow exchange students, executives who often change accommodation for professional reasons or fans of decoration to have access to furniture rental according to their desires.With the expansion of human movement and the growing desire to change furniture is no longer necessarily relevant and this is why renting is a matter of course. With the desire to offer furniture for everyone, RentEasy is accessible. In the form of monthly or annual packages, everything is possible and adaptable to everyone's profile. Moreover, this mode of consumption is all the more responsible as it avoids throwing away and is part of an upcycling or closed circuit approach.
